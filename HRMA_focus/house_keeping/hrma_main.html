HRMA SRC2 Trends

The links below show various characteristics of detected imaging sources. Standard level 2 data products (*src2.fits) are used. Only observations with focus (sim_x) within 0.01 mm of nominal value for given SI configuration are used. Since many HRC observations in early years have a larger defocus value, there are almost no HRC observations in years 1999 and 2000. Sources are also filtered for strength (SNR > 6). Extended sources are eliminated by rejecting sources wider than 15 pixels for ACIS and 500 pixels for HRC.

  • X,Y - position plots of detected sources
  • PSF - point spread function vs. off-axis distance
  • Roundness - roundness metric of source ellipse (major axis / minor axis) vs. off-axis distance
  • EE_Radius - encircled energy radius vs. off-axis distance (with celldetect artifacts)
  • SNR - signal-to-noise ratio of detected sources vs. off-axis distance; used to filter out poor quality sources
  • Rotation - Rotation angle of source region; rotation agngle (rotang) vs. angle computed from x-y positions (angd)
  • Rotation2 - Rotang/Angd vs. off-axis distance
